CompSoc is Durham University’s Computing Society. It is run entirely by students and serves to promote programming, development and general computing. We are a community of passionate problem solvers who come together to run weekly meetings, workshops and hackathons throughout the year. No matter if you're a novice or expert, come down to see what we have to offer!
